# Awards

Awards are a way to recognize exceptional service and achievements which can incentivize participation and boost
morale.

## How to create an award

To create an award, go to Awards (Admin Panel > MILHQ > Organization > Awards) and click the + button.

![Awards](/user-manual/plugins/milhq-plugin/awards.png)

- **Name**: The name of the award.
- **Description**: Brief summary outlining the award, could include the requirements for it.
- **Image**: Visual ribbon or medal representing the award, displayed on p-files.

## How to award users

To award soldiers, go to Soldiers (Admin Panel > MILHQ > Soldiers) and click on the Quick Actions button and select
New Award

![AwardSoldier](/user-manual/plugins/milhq-plugin/award-soldier.png)

- **Soldiers**: Here you can select an individual soldier or multiple.
- **Created At**: Date of record.
- **Rank**: The award you'd like to award.
- **Text**: If you want to supply a custom message which is displayed on the p-file.
- **Document**: Attach a document to the record.

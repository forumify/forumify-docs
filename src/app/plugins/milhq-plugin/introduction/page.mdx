# Introduction

Welcome to forumify's first party unit and personnel management plugin, MILHQ.

Designed specifically for Military Simulation units and structured communities, MILHQ serves as a comprehensive backend
for your organization.
It seamlessly integrates advanced personnel tracking, chain of command management, and operational logistics directly
into your forumify community.

This documentation serves to provide you with a complete guide through the plugin, from initial setup to managing
complex features.

# Installation

Package name for manual installation: `forumify/forumify-milhq-plugin`
